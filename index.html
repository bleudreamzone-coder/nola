Secondary pathway covering 35% of drugs missed by CYP450-only checkers. Critical for opioids, NSAIDs, antidepressants.

Drug efflux protein affecting blood-brain barrier penetration. CBD inhibits P-gp — increases CNS exposure of psychiatric meds 20-40%.
